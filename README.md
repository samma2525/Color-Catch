#Color Catch! 
You are a little girl in some village, but suddenly something dark took the color from the village, now you try to bring the color back to your town!


#How do the levels go:
##Stage 1:
###World 1: 
Level 1: normal collecting to droplets
Level 2: bombs added
Level 3: colorful normal collecting
Level 4: bomb and colorful (5) droplets only
Level 5: bomb and colorful (10) droplets

###World 2: (map changed)
Level 1: 2 bombs - no color
Level 2: 2 bombs - more collecting
Level 3: colorful + bombs
Level 4: colorful + bombs
Level 5: colorful + bombs

###World 3:(map changed)
Patrolling enemies are introduced, with different levels of color changing and bombs.

##Stage 2: 
Simple 2D platformer to go to the late statue that will finish the game
